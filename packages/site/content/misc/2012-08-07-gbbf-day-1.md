Today is the start of the [Great British Beer Festival](http://gbbf.org.uk), relocated for 2012 to its old home of Kensington Olympia due to some Olympian Volleyballists laying claim to Earl’s Court.

Some technical problems with my [CAMRA](http://www.camra.org.uk) membership (expired membership card) made entry a bit more tedious than necessary but once in I have to admit I much prefer Olympia as a venue to Earls Court.

Beers tasted today (in chronological order):

1. [Hogs Back](http://www.hogsback.co.uk) Summer Ale
2. [Dark Star](http://darkstarbrewing.co.uk) American Pale Ale (Bronze Medal - Overall)
3. [Green Jack](http://www.green-jack.com) Trawlerboys Best Bitter (Silver Medal - Overall, Gold Medal - Best Bitters)
4. [Cheddar Crown & Glory](http://www.cheddarales.co.uk/crownandglory.php)
5. [Kissingate](http://www.kissingate.co.uk/Welcome.html) Black Cherry Mild
6. [Sierra Nevada](http://www.sierranevada.com) Torpedo Extra Pale (on cask)
7. [Dark Star](http://darkstarbrewing.co.uk) Festival (Gold Medal - Strong Bitters)
8. [Orgasmic Parton Perry](http://www.ratebeer.com/beer/orgasmic-parton-perry/135174/) (at TC’s insistence)

Hoping to continue the beer journey later this week..
