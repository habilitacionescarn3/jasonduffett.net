The morning after [Stone Brewery](/misc/beer-garden-of-eden/) started off with a fender-bender in the car park. A [giant, black Challenger](http://beerambassadors.blogspot.com/2010/06/car.html) that was honking incessantly wasn't conspicuous enough as someone very slowly backed into us. Still, it was a small hiccup in the schedule. We needed to get moving to make our 12 noon appointment at San Diego's [Ballast Point Brewing](http://ballastpointbrewingandspirits.com/Welcome.html).

As always, all the beer nerd facts are available at [BeerAmbassadors.com](http://beerambassadors.blogspot.com/2010/06/west-coast-ipas.html).

Earl and Matt at Ballast Point were great and spent a lot of time chatting with us and giving us a tour of their brewery and distillery.

![Chalkboard beer menu listing styles, ABV and prices at Ballast Point](/assets/posts/8275268147069768752/0.jpg)

Here's a copy of their menu.  
At the time they'd completely run out of their award-winning [Sculpin IPA](http://ballastpointbrewingandspirits.com/seasonal_beers.html), but Matt took us across San Diego to the home brew mart where the brewery originally started where they still had 1 last keg on tap.

After Ballast Point we drove to [Green Flash](http://www.greenflashbrew.com/), especially loved their IPA.

All the beer details at [http://beerambassadors.blogspot.com/2010/06/west-coast-ipas.html](http://beerambassadors.blogspot.com/2010/06/west-coast-ipas.html).

We rounded off our last day in southern California watching the Lakers game at [Pizza Port](http://www.pizzaport.com/) in Calsbad.

The next morning we did a little celebrity-watching when we spotted [Mark "The Poo" Philippoussis](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mark_Philippoussis) enjoying a breakfast burrito at [Pipes Cafe](http://www.pipescafe.com/Home.htm) in Cardiff.

So long California, next stop - Denver...

_Beer Ambassadors at large, Ballast Point Brewery._
